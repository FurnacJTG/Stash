A curated archive of video game ROMs focused primarily on USA-region releases. Games that were released exclusively outside the USA or that contain significant regional differences may also be included.



#### Region Policy



The archive primarily contains USA-region ROMs. Exceptions may be included for:



• Games released exclusively outside the USA

• Titles with major regional differences

• Historically significant alternate revisions or versions



Examples include USA and Japan titles such as Sonic CD and Castlevania III.
